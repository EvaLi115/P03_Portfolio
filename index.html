ABOUT

I am a programmer who is passionate about programming with interactive systems, especially for both web and mobile. While completing my Bachelor of Science degree in the School of Interactive Art and Technology (SIAT) at SFU, I learnt various coding languages (such as C, C Sharp, Java and HTML), user-centred design processes, and different prototyping software (such as Axure, InVision, and Proto.io). With these pieces of knowledge, I was able to complete some team projects focusing on user-centred visual and experience design.
